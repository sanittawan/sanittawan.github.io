About

Hi! I am a recent graduate of the University of Chicago's Computational Social Science program. Prior to UChicago, I worked as a due diligence researcher at Kroll and earned an MA from Johns Hopkins SAIS and a BA from the Faculty of Political Science, Chulalongkorn University in Bangkok, Thailand.

Research Interests

My main research interests are in democratization, democratic breakdowns, democratic erosion, authoritarian politics, information control, censorship, effects of persuasion on voting and election, party formation, and computational tools used in political science research. I recently developed interest in Athenian democracy and republicanism.
